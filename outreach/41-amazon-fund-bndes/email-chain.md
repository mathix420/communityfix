# Email Chain — Amazon Fund Department / BNDES

## Message 1 — Initial outreach

**To:** fundoamazonia@bndes.gov.br
**Subject:** Amazon Fund featured as a reference case study on CommunityFix

---

Dear Amazon Fund Department,

I am writing to let you know that the Amazon Fund (Fundo Amazônia) is documented as a reference case study on CommunityFix (communityfix.org), an open platform mapping real-world solutions to environmental and social challenges.

The case study focuses on the Fund's performance-based model — where verified, independently-audited reductions in Amazon deforestation trigger payments from international donors such as Norway and Germany — and highlights it as a successful example of results-based forest finance at national scale.

The idea behind CommunityFix is simple: by documenting what was actually tried — and what came of it, costs and pitfalls included — other cities, regions and countries can avoid the same mistakes and go straight to the solutions that work. It's a small but real contribution to a better world: turning one community's hard-won experience into knowledge everyone can build on.

We would be grateful for two things:

1. **Accuracy review** — If anything in the case study is incomplete, outdated, or imprecise, we would welcome your corrections. You can view it directly at communityfix.org.
2. **Enrichment** — If you are willing to share additional details — metrics on deforestation avoided, funded project areas, or lessons learned — we can update the record to make it more useful for practitioners and policymakers looking to replicate the model elsewhere.

CommunityFix is a non-commercial reference resource. Our goal is simply to make proven approaches easier to discover and adapt.

Thank you for the Fund's work, and I hope to hear from you.

Best regards,

Arnaud Gissinger
Founder — CommunityFix · communityfix.org
