# Email Chain — Case Study 54: Medellín Green Corridors

---

## Message 1 — Initial outreach

**To:** atencion.ciudadana@medellin.gov.co  
**Subject:** Medellín's green corridors featured on CommunityFix — would you review it?

---

Dear Marcela,

My name is Arnaud Gissinger and I am the founder of CommunityFix (communityfix.org), an open platform that documents real-world solutions to urban and environmental challenges so that practitioners everywhere can learn from them and replicate what works.

I am writing because we have documented Medellín's network of 30+ interconnected green corridors as a reference case study on our platform. The programme's results — measurable temperature reductions at corridor sites, delivered at a cost of roughly $6.50 per resident — make it one of the most compelling examples of urban heat mitigation we have encountered, and one we believe many other cities could learn from.

Before we publish it more widely, we would very much like to:

1. **Ensure accuracy** — if anything in our case study misrepresents the programme, the Secretaría's work, or the outcomes, we want to correct it.
2. **Enrich the record** — if you or your team are willing to share additional context, metrics, lessons learned, or links to official reports, we would be glad to include them.

The idea behind CommunityFix is simple: by documenting what was actually tried — and what came of it, costs and pitfalls included — other cities, regions and countries can avoid the same mistakes and go straight to the solutions that work. It's a small but real contribution to a better world: turning one community's hard-won experience into knowledge everyone can build on.

You can view the case study here: https://communityfix.org

Would you or a colleague have 15–20 minutes to take a look and share any feedback? There is no commercial angle here — CommunityFix is a public-interest project and the platform is free.

Thank you for the extraordinary work the Secretaría de Medio Ambiente has done to make Medellín a reference point for urban greening worldwide.

Warm regards,

Arnaud Gissinger  
Founder — CommunityFix · communityfix.org
