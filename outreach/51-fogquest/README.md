## Notes

The case study documents a failure outcome — the fog collectors at El Tofo supplied Chungungo with water for years, but the project eventually collapsed when maintenance responsibilities were not effectively transferred to the local community and institutional support waned. FogQuest was founded partly in response to lessons learned from this experience, making Schemenauer both a primary source and a thoughtful steward of the project's legacy. Tone should be respectful and acknowledge the technical achievement while framing the failure honestly as a learning resource.
