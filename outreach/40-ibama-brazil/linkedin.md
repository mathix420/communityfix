The idea behind CommunityFix is simple: by documenting what was actually tried — and what came of it, costs and pitfalls included — other governments and organisations can avoid the same mistakes and go straight to the approaches that work. It's a small but real contribution to a better world: turning one country's hard-won experience into knowledge everyone can build on.
