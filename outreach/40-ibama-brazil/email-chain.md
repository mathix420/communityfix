## Message 1 — Initial outreach

**To:** imprensa@mma.gov.br
**Subject:** IBAMA's Amazon enforcement documented on CommunityFix — review invited

---

Dear IBAMA Communications Team,

My name is Arnaud Gissinger, and I am the founder of CommunityFix (communityfix.org), an open platform that documents real-world solutions to environmental and social challenges so that other governments, organisations, and communities can learn from them and adapt what works.

We have published a case study on IBAMA's reinvigorated Amazon enforcement programme — the coordinated effort by IBAMA, ICMBio, INPE, and the Ministry of Environment and Climate Change that helped bring deforestation in the Legal Amazon to an 11-year low. The case study covers the operational approach, the role of satellite monitoring data from INPE, the scale of fines and seizures, and the measurable outcome.

The idea behind CommunityFix is simple: by documenting what was actually tried — and what came of it, costs and pitfalls included — other cities, regions and countries can avoid the same mistakes and go straight to the solutions that work. It's a small but real contribution to a better world: turning one community's hard-won experience into knowledge everyone can build on.

I am writing for two reasons:

1. **Accuracy review.** We want the record to reflect what actually happened on the ground. If your team spots anything that needs correcting or updating, we will make the changes promptly.
2. **Enrichment.** If there are published reports, enforcement statistics, or lessons learned that you are willing to share, we would be glad to link to them so that practitioners in other countries can draw on IBAMA's experience directly.

The case study is publicly accessible and freely reusable under an open licence. There is no commercial interest on our part — the goal is simply to make proven environmental enforcement approaches visible and replicable.

I would be happy to share the direct link to the case study and answer any questions. Please feel free to reply to this email or reach out via communityfix.org.

Thank you for your time and for the work IBAMA continues to do for the Amazon.

Best regards,

Arnaud Gissinger
Founder — CommunityFix · communityfix.org
