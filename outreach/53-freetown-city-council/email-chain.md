## Message 1 — Initial outreach

**To:** info@fcc.gov.sl
**Subject:** #FreetownTheTreeTown is featured on CommunityFix — would love your input

---

Dear Mayor Aki-Sawyerr,

I hope this finds you well.

I'm Arnaud Gissinger, founder of CommunityFix (communityfix.org) — a platform that maps real-world solutions to civic and environmental challenges so that other cities and communities can learn from them and replicate what works.

We've documented the **#FreetownTheTreeTown** programme as a reference case study on the platform. The initiative stands out for its combination of the Greenstand TreeTracker app and survival-based payments to community planters — an approach that addresses both the ecological and economic dimensions of urban reforestation in a way few cities have managed.

I'm writing for two reasons:

1. **Accuracy** — we want to make sure every detail we've published reflects the programme faithfully. If anything needs correcting or updating, we'd be grateful for your team's input.
2. **Enrichment** — if there are metrics, lessons learned, or sources you'd like to add (planting targets, tree-survival rates, number of community members involved, etc.), we'd love to include them so that policymakers researching similar programmes have the fullest possible picture.

The idea behind CommunityFix is simple: by documenting what was actually tried — and what came of it, costs and pitfalls included — other cities, regions and countries can avoid the same mistakes and go straight to the solutions that work. It's a small but real contribution to a better world: turning one community's hard-won experience into knowledge everyone can build on.

The case study is live and publicly accessible. I'm happy to share the direct link or arrange a brief call at your convenience.

Thank you for everything Freetown is doing — #FreetownTheTreeTown is exactly the kind of initiative other cities need to hear about.

Warm regards,

**Arnaud Gissinger**
Founder — CommunityFix · communityfix.org
