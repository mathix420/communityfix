## Message 1 — Initial outreach

**To:** CEMO-Office@lacity.org  
**Subject:** Los Angeles cool-surface program documented on CommunityFix — your review invited

---

Dear Ms. Segura,

My name is Arnaud Gissinger and I am the founder of CommunityFix (communityfix.org), an open platform that documents real-world solutions to civic and environmental challenges so that other cities and communities can learn from and replicate them.

I am writing to let you know that Los Angeles's cool-surface program — combining reflective road coatings with cool-roof requirements under the Sustainable City pLAn, with its numeric urban-rural temperature-reduction target — is now documented as a reference case study on CommunityFix.

We believe the work your office has led under the Climate Emergency Mobilization Office is precisely the kind of implemented, evidence-based intervention that practitioners in other cities are looking for. Making it discoverable and replicable is the core purpose of the platform.

The idea behind CommunityFix is simple: by documenting what was actually tried — and what came of it, costs and pitfalls included — other cities, regions and countries can avoid the same mistakes and go straight to the solutions that work. It's a small but real contribution to a better world: turning one community's hard-won experience into knowledge everyone can build on.

I would be very grateful if you (or a colleague from your office) could:

1. **Review the case study for accuracy** — confirm that the programme description, scope, and current status reflect the facts as you know them.
2. **Enrich it with recent figures or lessons** — any updated metrics (e.g., lane-miles coated, temperature reductions measured, cost per square foot), recent lessons learned, or photos you are comfortable sharing publicly would make the record far more useful to replicators.

The case study is openly accessible and editable through the platform. If it is easier, you are also welcome to reply to this email with any corrections or additions and I will update it on your behalf.

Thank you for the climate leadership your office continues to demonstrate. I hope CommunityFix can help amplify its reach.

Warm regards,

Arnaud Gissinger  
Founder — CommunityFix · communityfix.org
