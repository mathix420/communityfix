## Message 1 — Initial Outreach

**To:** community.energy@bpu.nj.gov
**Subject:** Newark's Urban Heat Island program is documented on CommunityFix — would you review it?

Dear President Guhl-Sadovy,

My name is Arnaud Gissinger, and I'm the founder of CommunityFix (communityfix.org) — an open platform that documents real-world solutions to urban and environmental challenges so that other cities and communities can learn from them and replicate what works.

I wanted to reach out because we have documented Newark's $5M Urban Heat Island Mitigation Program — covering the combination of tree canopy expansion, cool pavement, green infrastructure, and smart-misting deployed in response to record heat — as a reference case study on the platform.

I'm writing with two requests:

1. **Accuracy review.** We want to make sure the details we have on file reflect what NJBPU has actually implemented. If anything is incorrect or out of date, we would be grateful for your corrections.

2. **Enrichment.** If you are willing to share recent figures, outcome data, lessons learned, or photos from the programme, we can add them to the case study. That additional depth is precisely what helps practitioners in other cities decide whether — and how — to adapt a similar approach.

The idea behind CommunityFix is simple: by documenting what was actually tried — and what came of it, costs and pitfalls included — other cities, regions and countries can avoid the same mistakes and go straight to the solutions that work. It's a small but real contribution to a better world: turning one community's hard-won experience into knowledge everyone can build on.

The case study is publicly accessible and will remain freely available with full credit to NJBPU. There is no commercial angle — CommunityFix is built around the idea that public-interest knowledge should travel as far as possible.

Would you or a member of your team be open to a brief exchange? I am happy to work around your schedule and can share the current draft for your review at any point.

Thank you for everything NJBPU is doing to protect Newark residents from extreme heat.

Warm regards,

Arnaud Gissinger
Founder — CommunityFix · communityfix.org
