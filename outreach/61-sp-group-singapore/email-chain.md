I'm reaching out for two reasons:

2. **Enrichment** — If your team is willing, additional data — such as efficiency metrics, total cooling capacity, the timeline of network expansion, or lessons learned — would make the case study significantly more useful to others considering similar infrastructure.

There is no cost or commitment involved. If it's helpful, I'm happy to share the draft directly.

The idea behind CommunityFix is simple: by documenting what was actually tried — and what came of it, costs and pitfalls included — other cities, regions and countries can avoid the same mistakes and go straight to the solutions that work. It's a small but real contribution to a better world: turning one community's hard-won experience into knowledge everyone can build on.

Would someone on your team have 15–20 minutes to take a look?

Thank you for the work SP Group has done in making Marina Bay a model for sustainable urban cooling.
